Our Story

Starting a bakery was Rosemary's childhood dream. She grew up in the kitchen baking all kinds of recipes with family and friends. What started as a fun pastime quickly became her passion. After graduating from the College of Charleston with a bachelor's in hospitality, she was still thinking about starting that bakery. Rosemary aspired to attend p√¢tisserie school, eager to expand her professional knowledge and skills. In 2019, she was accepted into Edinburgh School of Food and Wine and packed up to learn from the best for 12 weeks in Scotland. Shortly after returning to the U.S., Rosemary's bakery was founded. It quickly became the most popular spot in town for delicious baked goods and delectable coffee.

Baked Goods

Rosemary's is all about fresh ingredients. We offer a variety of loaves, cakes, and other sweet treats baked early each morning. Of course, our bakers double as baristas! Try our signature drinks made with homemade syrups and Cognoscenti beans.
